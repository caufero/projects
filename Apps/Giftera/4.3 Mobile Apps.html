Giftera Mobile Apps Content Spec (iOS + Android)

Version: v1.0
Scope: All mobile app screens, labels, buttons, empty states, confirmations, errors, and system messages.
Tone: Clear, premium, human. Short sentences.
Currency: GHS
Time: GMT
Primary identity: Mobile number

Mobile Apps Rules

  1. Use clear verbs: Send, Pay, Confirm, Save, Resend.
  2. Always show fees before payment.
  3. Always show what the receiver will get: Card link + SMS fallback.
  4. Confirm destructive actions: delete contact, remove favourite, cancel draft.
  5. Keep buttons under 22 characters where possible.
  6. Use line icons and gradients for premium feel (design note, not copy).
