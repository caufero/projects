BUSINESS PLAN

Digital Messaging + MoMo Gift Delivery Platform Gold, Silver, Blue, Yellow & Miscellaneous Collections

1. Executive Summary

This business provides a digital gifting platform where senders purchase beautifully designed message templates organized into Gold, Silver, Blue, Yellow, and Miscellaneous collections, and include a MoMo cash gift that is delivered instantly to the recipient. The recipient receives a personalised heartfelt message, a MoMo alert with the gifted amount, and real-time delivery confirmation. The sender pays for: template, gift amount, and a platform service fee (percentage commission).

2. Business Description

Company Overview:

A digital greeting and gifting service merging creativity, technology, and fintech.

Vision:

Become Ghana’s leading digital gifting platform.

Mission:

Provide secure, seamless, meaningful message-and-gift experiences through curated templates and secure payments.

Objectives:

100,000 users in 12 months, 1M transactions in 3 years, partnerships with telcos, fintechs, and expansion to Africa & diaspora.

3. Market Analysis

Mobile money transactions in Ghana exceed GHS 1 trillion annually. The platform taps into MoMo usage, digital lifestyle trends, youth gifting culture, and diaspora micro-gifting.

Target Market: Young adults, couples, corporate HR, diaspora, event organisers.

Market Need: No platform currently combines digital messages + MoMo gifts in one experience.

4. Products & Services

Colour Collections:

Gold, Silver, Blue, Yellow, Misc.

Custom Messages:

Users personalise texts, backgrounds, or photos.

MoMo Gift Delivery:

Instant mobile money transfer.

Service Fee Model:

5% commission per transaction.

Premium Features:

Scheduled delivery, priority delivery, music/voice notes, corporate packages.

5. Revenue Model

  • Template sales (avg GHS 35)
  • 5% transaction commission (avg GHS 4)
  • Premium features
  • Corporate API
  • Ads

6. Financial Projections

Year 1 Revenue: GHS 2,135,250 Year 1 Costs: GHS 1,320,000 Year 1 Profit: GHS 815,250

3-Year Projection shows strong growth to GHS 5–46M revenue in Year 3.

7. Marketing & Growth Strategy

Digital marketing, influencers, WhatsApp broadcasts, corporate partnerships, referral system, seasonal campaigns.

8. Operations Plan

Technology Stack:

React/Flutter, Node/Python backend, MoMo API, AWS, real-time tracking.

Human Resources:

Designers, developers, support, operations, marketing.

Customer Support:

24/7 chat, AI FAQ.

9. Risk Analysis

Risks:

MoMo API delays, cybersecurity, fraud, competition.

Mitigation:

Redundant payment channels, fraud detection, branding, influencers.

10. Investment Requirement

Total needed: GHS 1.5M

Allocation:

  • Tech 35%
  • Marketing 30%
  • Templates 10%
  • Talent 10%
  • Operations 15%

11. Investor Returns

Option A: 15% equity Option B: 7% revenue share for 5 years Option C: 12% convertible note

12. Conclusion

The platform is positioned to dominate Ghana’s digital gifting space with strong profitability and scalability.
