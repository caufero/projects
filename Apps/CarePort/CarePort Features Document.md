# CarePort Features Overview

## 1. About This Document
<div class="feature-row">
  <div class="feature-col">
    <p>This document explains what CarePort can do for a hospital.</p>
    <p>It is written for hospital owners and managers, so you can understand it fast and decide if it fits your work.</p>
    <p>It also includes screenshots of images of the system.</p>
  </div>

## 2. What CarePort Does
<div class="feature-row feature-row-rev">
  <img class="feature-img-tall" src="Screenshots/2. Side Menu.png" alt="Side Menu" />

  <div class="feature-col-wider">
    <p>CarePort is one system that helps a hospital handle patient work and also handle internal hospital work.</p>
    <p>With CarePort, you can:</p>
    <ul>
      <li>Register patients and keep their history</li>
      <li>Manage walk-ins and appointments</li>
      <li>Run consultations, vitals, diagnosis, lab, prescriptions, and treatments</li>
      <li>Bill patients, take payments, and print reports</li>
      <li>Manage medicines stock and hospital purchasing</li>
      <li>Manage staff records, attendance, leave, and payroll setup</li>
      <li>Manage inventory and procurement of items</li>
    </ul>
  </div>
</div>

## 5. Modules At A Glance

| Module               | What It Does                                                    | Who Uses It           |
| -------------------- | --------------------------------------------------------------- | --------------------- |
| Patient Registration | Create and manage patient records                               | Front desk, admin     |
| Walk-Ins             | Handle patients who come without booking                        | Front desk            |
| Appointments         | Book and track today, past, and future appointments             | Front desk, admin     |
| Visits               | Capture vitals, notes, diagnosis, services, treatment, and more | Nurses, doctors       |
| Lab                  | Send lab requests, enter results, attach lab documents          | Lab staff, doctors    |
| Prescriptions        | Record prescriptions for a visit                                | Doctors               |
| Treatments           | Track treatment administrations and treatment chart             | Nurses                |
| Billing and Payments | Bill patients, receive payments, manage discounts               | Cashier, admin        |
| Medicines Stock      | Manage stock, add stock, adjust stock, check available quantity | Pharmacy, storekeeper |
| Procurement          | Create purchase orders, submit, approve, and report             | Admin, procurement    |
| Inpatient            | Admit, manage inpatient notes and vitals, track discharge       | Nurses, doctors       |
| Reports              | Generate and print hospital reports                             | Management, admin     |
| Staff Management     | Staff records, attendance, leave requests, appraisals           | Admin, HR, management |
| Payroll Setup        | Payroll items and payroll grouping                              | Admin, HR, management |



## 6. How A Normal Hospital Day Runs
<div class="link-grid">
  <div class="link-card">
    <div class="link-title">1. Patient Arrives</div> <br>
    <ul>
      <li>Staff finds the patient or registers a new patient.</li>
      <li>If the patient did not book, staff records it as a walk-in.</li>
    </ul>
  </div>

  <div class="link-card">
    <div class="link-title">2. Appointment Checks</div> <br>
    <ul>
      <li>Staff can see today’s appointments and handle them in order.</li>
    </ul>
  </div>

  <div class="link-card">
    <div class="link-title">3. Visit Starts</div> <br>
    <ul>
      <li>Nurse captures vitals.</li>
      <li>Doctor writes notes and diagnosis.</li>
      <li>Doctor requests lab if needed.</li>
      <li>Doctor adds prescription if needed.</li>
      <li>Staff adds services or treatments done.</li>
    </ul>
  </div>

  <div class="link-card">
    <div class="link-title">4. Lab Work</div> <br>
    <ul>
      <li>Lab sees pending requests.</li>
      <li>Lab enters results and can attach result documents.</li>
      <li>Doctor can review results.</li>
    </ul>
  </div>

  <div class="link-card">
    <div class="link-title">5. Billing & Payment</div> <br>
    <ul>
      <li>Billing is done based on what the hospital provided.</li>
      <li>Cashier receives payment and can print receipts or reports.</li>
    </ul>
  </div>

  <div class="link-card">
    <div class="link-title">6. End of Day</div> <br>
    <ul>
      <li>Management generates reports and reviews performance.</li>
    </ul>
  </div>
</div>
