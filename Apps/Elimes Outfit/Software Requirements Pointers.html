Section 2

Daily Operational Workflow

The end-to-end daily flow across inventory, bespoke, production, store actions, and payroll.

Inventory Flow
Workflow
Store Management
  1. Enter new stock
  2. Assign to Store
  3. Sales / Transfer / Adjustments
  4. Closing Stock
Warehouse Management
  1. Enter new stock
Bespoke Order + Production Flow
Workflow
Sales Rep / Store
  • Create bespoke order
  • Measurements + Deposits
  • Assign production job
Factory / Admin
  • Assign Tailor
  • Issue Materials to Tailors
Tailor
  • Daily work log
  • Update job statuses
  • Make completion
Store
  • Receive outfits
  • Customer Pickup
  • Sell
  • Close Order
Commission & Payroll Flow
Workflow
System
  1. Generate monthly sales
  2. Apply commission rules
  3. Generate salary due
Admin / Finance
  1. Review and approve
  2. Record payment of salaries
Section 3

Core System Modules

The modules and report requirements the system must include, plus non-negotiable constraints.

Modules
Core
  • User Roles / Permissions
  • Inventory Management
    • Material costing
  • Bespoke Orders + Customer Measurements
  • Production Task Management + Tailor Daily Logs
  • Sales Recording + Store Level Inventory
  • Commission & Salary Automation
  • Financial Tracking
    • Revenue
    • Expenses
    • Profits
    • Reports & Analytics (Monthly & Generate)
    • Audit Trail & Approval Workflow
Key Reports Required
Reports
  1. Production Distribution (Abidjan, Ghana [Shop1 + Shop2 + Others])
  2. Bespoke vs Ready-to-wear production counts
  3. Bespoke vs Ready-to-wear revenue comparisons
  4. Store performance rankings
  5. Tailor productivity + Delivery timelines
  6. Sales staff commission and salary due list
  7. Monthly and yearly financial summaries
Non-negotiable System Requirements
Must-have
  • Multi-store support with centralized control
  • Role-based access and approvals
  • Automated commission & salary computations
  • Inventory traceability per store
  • Exportable reports
  • Secure audit logs for all actions
  • Dashboard for management performance tracking
